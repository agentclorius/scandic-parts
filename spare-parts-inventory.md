## ⚠️ Important Notes

1. **Verify part numbers before ordering** — Dell uses multiple sub-variants per model (e.g., with/without WWAN, touch/non-touch)
2. **Keyboard layouts** — ensure Nordic/DK layout. Backlit ≠ non-backlit (different part numbers)
3. **5450 RAM is soldered** — cannot be field-replaced. Motherboard swap only.
4. **5440/5450 use M.2 2230 SSDs** — not the same as 2280 in older models
5. **Source from Dell OEM or certified refurb** — aftermarket batteries are a fire risk
